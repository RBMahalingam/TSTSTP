Our Understanding

The Requests from CRG Data Capabilities

02

Four workstreams identified — Customer Pod, New Roles and Transition Roles are the focus of this staffing model

👥
Customer Pod
Establish and operate a dedicated POD for the CRG Data Capabilities Portfolio to centrally manage sponsor engagements. Scope includes intake governance, prioritization, execution oversight, quality & compliance control, and stakeholder transparency — delivered through a scalable, partner-led delivery structure.
👤
New Roles
Provide net new staffing to support emerging data domain and governance needs across CRG. Initial scope is focused on Celina's Platforms team — sourcing and onboarding key leads before expanding to broader team build-out across Platform, Data Domains, and Data Governance functions.
🔄
Transition Roles
Manage the structured transition of existing CRG roles to Accenture, covering two streams: (1) rebadging of current CRG staff, and (2) backfilling positions currently held by incumbent partners with Accenture-sourced resources aligned to the Platform domain.
⚙️
Ingestion Framework
Design, build, and operationalize a production-grade data ingestion framework supporting CRG's data platform — covering pipeline architecture, source connectivity, data quality standards, and scalable infrastructure. Scope to be aligned with Srikar.

Executive Summary

A Model Built for Scale, Speed & Quality

03

Accenture is proposing a model to support CRG Data's staffing needs — focusing on driving the rapid intake, prioritization, and delivery of sponsor projects via the development of an Engagement POD, which leverages the following principles…

🏛️
Strong Governance
Strengthen intake governance and prioritization while providing predictable execution and delivery transparency across the portfolio.
✅
Embedded Quality & Compliance
Ensure Data & AI quality, regulatory compliance, and architectural alignment through standardized delivery frameworks.
⚡
Leverage a Core / Flex Model
Bring the right resource, at the right time, for the right task — a shared pool serves steady-state demand while dedicated roles absorb segment-specific work.
🔁
Iterative Delivery & Optimization
Applies a co-design and co-development approach, rapidly testing and iterating solutions before optimizing for scale and impact across CRG workstreams.
👥
Dedicated, Sponsor-Centric POD with End-to-End Ownership
A cross-functional Customer POD acting as an extension of CRG Data, owning centralized sponsor intake and delivery.
